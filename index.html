About Melissa Hinson

Melissa Hinson was born in Memphis, Tennessee and has been writing music and singing for over 40 years. She’s spent more than 30 years working with kids, helping them build confidence, learn vocal control, and discover their unique sound. At The Song Shack, she blends heart, experience, and a love of teaching to bring out the best in every student — from first‑time singers to seasoned performers.
